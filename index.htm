Chicken Egg Farm is a farming simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more Chickens you have, the more eggs they find (each Chicken finds at a rate of 1 egg per day). Hatch more Chickens with your eggs to multiply your production, or cash them out for BNB!

Automated Market

Chicken Egg Farm features a high tech automated market that lets you instantly buy Chicken or sell Chicken Eggs. Driven by supply and demand, the price automatically adjusts as players trade.
